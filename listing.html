Wonderful large family home under the Mother City Sky

Wake up to majestic views of Table Mountain with this vintage style family home situated in the sought after Upper Oranjezicht area.

The six bedroom home sits perched on an elevated driveway with high walls, a large entrance gate and electric fencing before you enter the large property. A single garage with ample secure off-street parking makes use your car and those of your visitors park safely.

A front patio serves as your inlet to the home where you are greeted by wooden floors, sash windows and high ceilings. The lounge and dining feature a picturesque setting which is also air conditioned. You can immediately tell that this home is immaculately maintained.

Each bedroom is spacious all with en suite bathrooms, making it an ideal purchase for a large family or to run as a guest house. To the add to the already impressive home, there is flatlet with a separate entrance which can be used as domestic quarters. There is also a study area as well.

The swimming pool and outdoor yard area provide the perfect backdrop of Table Mountain. The home also sits close to a host of guest houses and public transportation routes.
